About Us – BoxClub NYC

Welcome to BoxClub NYC, where boxing is more than just a sport — it's a way to take control of your life.

At BoxClub NYC, we're here to help people get their fitness together, build their dream body, and sharpen their mind — all while learning the art of boxing for self-defense, confidence, and strength.

Whether you're looking to lose weight, tone up, compete, or simply push past your limits, we're here to guide you every step of the way. We believe in real results, real discipline, and a real community that supports you inside and outside the gym.

What We Offer

Full-Body Boxing Workouts

For all fitness levels - from beginners to advanced athletes

Strength & Conditioning

Focus on building physical and mental resilience

Self-Defense Training

Real-world techniques for confidence and protection

High-Energy Environment

Positive atmosphere that motivates and inspires

All Age Groups

Classes for youth, adults, and competitive fighters

Our Mission

"We don't just build fighters — we build people."

BoxClub NYC is a place where you'll train your body and mind, unlock your true potential, and leave every session better than when you walked in. Whether you're in it for fitness or for the fight, you're family here.
